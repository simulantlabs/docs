**Reports** turn weeks of simulation work into a recurring readout you can share. They live under **Intelligence → Reports**. Where the [Agent](/guides/agent) answers ad-hoc questions, reports are scheduled briefs — morning updates, weekly executive summaries, and sentiment watches — generated from your workspace's simulations, cohorts, and assets.

## Create a report

Go to **Intelligence → Reports** and pick a preset or start from scratch:

| Preset | Window | What it covers |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Morning brief** | Last 24 hours | What moved across your tests overnight |
| **Weekly executive summary** | Last 7 days | The week in review — results, cohort reactions, standout assets |
| **Sentiment watch** | Last 7 days | How sentiment is trending across cohorts and campaigns |
| **Custom report** | Your choice | Build your own section mix and schedule |

Each report can include:

- **Metrics** — headline numbers across recent runs
- **Takeaways** — the throughline in plain language
- **Sentiment** — how tone is shifting
- **Top tests** — the simulations that mattered most in the window
- **Verbatims** — standout quotes from the period

Toggle sections on or off when you configure a report. Set a **schedule** so editions arrive automatically, or click **Generate** to produce one on demand.

## Read an edition

Open any report to see its edition history. Each edition is a self-contained readout for the window it covers — metrics, takeaways, and verbatims you can print or share with stakeholders who were not in the app when the runs finished.

<Tip>
  Pair reports with [Memos](/guides/memos) for the narrative layer. Reports surface what changed; memos capture what you decided to do about it.
</Tip>
