An **asset** is the media a test or focus group step reacts to. Assets live in your workspace under **Library → Assets** so you can reuse them across simulations and wire them into test nodes on the Model canvas.

## Upload an asset

Go to **Assets → Upload asset** and drop in your file, or click to browse. Uploads run in the background and show progress as they go.

Supported formats include:

- **Video** — the most common asset for ad and hook testing.
- **Image** — static ads, key art, packaging, and stills.
- **Messaging** — headlines, captions, and body copy. You can upload a document or type copy directly in a test step.

<Note>
  Large video files are processed after upload so they play back smoothly in the app. An asset is ready to test once processing completes and its status shows **Ready**.
</Note>

## Use an asset in a simulation

On a simulation's **Model** tab, add a **Test** or **Focus group** step and connect an **Asset** node. To compare options, add up to four variants (A, B, C, D) and attach a different asset or message to each.

For copy tests, choose the **Messaging** format and paste the headline, caption, or body text for each variant — no upload required.

## Manage assets

Open **Assets** to see every file in the workspace, preview it, and see where it's been used. Reusing an asset across simulation steps keeps your results comparable and your library tidy.
