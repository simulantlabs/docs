**Memos** are where you write up what the simulation results mean. They sit in the **Intelligence** section of the sidebar alongside [Reports](/guides/reports) and the [Agent](/guides/agent) — the qualitative layer on top of the quantitative readouts.

## Personal and workspace memos

Open **Memos** and switch between two scopes:

- **Personal** — notes only you can see. Use them for draft thinking, meeting prep, or questions to take back to the model.
- **Workspace** — memos shared with everyone on the team. Use them for readouts, decision records, and campaign briefs.

Click **New memo** to start writing. Memos support rich text so you can structure a briefing with headings, lists, and emphasis — then link out to the simulations and cohorts the memo references.

<Tip>
  Write the memo after a simulation run finishes. State the hypothesis, what the result showed, and what you are doing next. Future-you (and your teammates) will thank you when the campaign moves on.
</Tip>
