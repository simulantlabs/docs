<Warning>
  **News feeds** were retired as a standalone product surface. The **Feeds** section no longer appears in the sidebar.
</Warning>

## What replaced them

Current-events context is now configured directly on a simulation's **Model** canvas:

1. Open a simulation's **Model** tab.
2. Click **Add node → News**.
3. Configure keywords to pull matching articles.
4. Wire the News node to a **Poll** or **Test** step's **Context** port.

The articles you select are passed to every persona in the run, so their reactions reflect the news your audience has actually been reading. Because articles are stored with the step, a run stays reproducible — you can revisit the exact context the panel saw, even weeks later when the news cycle has moved on.

<Tip>
  Keep keyword sets narrow. Two or three sharp terms produce a cleaner shortlist than one catch-all query. Create separate News nodes per topic — cost of living, election coverage, a specific competitor — rather than a single feed that tries to cover everything.
</Tip>
