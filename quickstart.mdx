This guide takes you from an empty workspace to your first test result. It should take about an hour, most of which is the simulation running.

## Prerequisites

- Access to Simulant at [app.simulant.tech](https://app.simulant.tech). If you don't have an account, [book a demo](https://simulant.tech/contact?intent=demo) or accept a workspace invite.
- A creative to test — a video, an image, or a block of copy. You can also start with messaging typed directly into the app.

## Set up your workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.simulant.tech/signup](https://app.simulant.tech/signup) with your work email, then enter the 6-digit code we send you. If you were invited to a workspace, open the invite link instead and accept.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Tell us your name, role, organization, and what you plan to use Simulant for. This creates your first **workspace** — the shared space where your team's cohorts, tests, and data live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the demo content">
    New workspaces come pre-populated with sample tests, cohorts, focus groups, and reports so you can click around before running anything of your own. When you're ready to work with just your own data, open the **Get started** checklist in the sidebar and select **Clear the demo content** — every list view switches to your real workspace state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The **Get started** checklist in the sidebar tracks your first-run steps — create a cohort, run a test, upload an asset, add a dataset, invite your team, and clear the demo content. Steps auto-complete as you do them, and the card retires itself once everything's checked off.
</Tip>

## Run your first test

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add data (optional)">
    Simulant ships with a **public library** of grounding datasets, so you can start immediately. To add your own signal, go to **Datasets → Add dataset**. See [Datasets](/data/datasets).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build a cohort">
    Go to **Cohorts → New cohort**. Name it, choose the source data it's built from, then narrow it with demographic filters and psychographic tags. Simulant shows the sample size (`n`) as you refine. See [Build a cohort](/guides/cohorts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your creative">
    Go to **Creatives → Upload creative** and drop in your video or image. For copy tests, you can skip this and type the messaging directly into the test builder. See [Creatives](/guides/creatives).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the test">
    Go to **Tests → New test**. Give it a name and a **hypothesis**, choose the format (Video, Image, or Messaging), add your variants, pick the cohort, and select the metrics and questions you want answered. See [Run a test](/guides/tests).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run it">
    Click **Run test**. Simulant questions the population and assembles the results. You'll get a notification when the run finishes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the results">
    Open the test to see headline metrics (recall, sentiment, intent, clarity), sentiment by segment, and tabs for **Verbatims**, **Themes**, and **Recommendations**. Use **Export** or **Share** to bring others in.

    <Frame caption="Your first result — headline metrics, sentiment by segment, and the verbatims behind the numbers.">
      <img src="/images/app/test-results.png" alt="Test results page showing metrics, segment sentiment, and voter verbatims" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  You've run your first simulation. Next, try convening a [focus group](/guides/focus-groups) from the same cohort to hear the room deliberate.
</Check>
